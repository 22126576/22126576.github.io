Installing and Maintaining Computer Hardware

Understanding and Managing Physical Components of IT Systems Content:
This unit dives into the world of computer hardware, including the types and functions of various components like processors, memory, storage devices, and peripherals. It also covers how to assemble, configure, and maintain hardware. Students will also explore best practices in hardware safety, upgrading systems, and the impact of hardware on overall system performance.
